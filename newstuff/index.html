PTW Respawn is the perfect place for your PTW!

We are a part of Greenstrikes, an Airsoft Store in Lisbon, so you'll find highly qualified personel and all sorts of items for your Airsoft needs. Having many years of experience, here you can service, upgrade repair or even buy a brand new Airsoft Training Weapon. We carry brands like Systema, Celcius, FCC, MAG, Etiny and much more.

Basic Service

Our inhouse expert will do a complete chekup on your gun, including the motor. He'll relube and tune everything.

Upgrades

Do you think that your gun needs something extra? We'll enhance it. From range to reliability.

Custom Builds

Is your gun too bland? We'll take care of it. From internal to external parts, we're prepared to build it for you!

Paint Jobs

Need your gun to blend in? Tell us you want it to look and we'll paint it.
