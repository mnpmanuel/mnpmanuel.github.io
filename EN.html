PTW Mosfet

The mosfet upgrade for your TW.

With a brand new and innovative design, this mosfet is made to increase the performance maintaining the reliability. Engineered to keep working without problems where others break, this mosfet isn't a just a replacement part, it's an upgrade.With it's size it makes it possible to carry the battery inside the buffer-tube. It's compatible with Systema, Celcius and G&D (newer generations) branded TWs. Functional prototype in test fase.

Hop Up Tracer Unit

Improve your night games

Always wanted to have a tracer without a supressor?
Supressors with tracer are expensive and give away your position due to the flash that it uses to light up the bbs. That's why we're developing an universal Hop Up tracer unit, that will outperform the supressor units and that requires the minimum amount of tools and modifications. Project that is being studied and developed as we speak.

Hop Up Patch

Increase your gun's range and accuracy with ease

Do you know what a R-Hop is? The R-Hop is a rubber that is placed on the inner barrel window that improves range and accurcy and that gives you the possibility of using heavier bbs.
You already knew about it but never took a chance assembling one? As we've already assemble many R-Hops, we understand that installing it might be tricky and time consuming (in some cases can reach several hours to get ir right). That's why we're developing a solution that allows you to buy a pre-assembled barrel+r-hop set or that only takes 5 minutes to install. Working prototype being currently submited to stress and durability tests.
