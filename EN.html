Regular Mosfet

A simple, high quality and low cost option for performance.

This mosfet stops the trigger contacts from beeing burnt with regular use. Even with a low cost, it was made with the best components available to date and designed to last while increasing performance above the average. It increases ROF (rate of fire), trigger response and it's easy to install. This is a tried and true solution, tested and installed in several AEGs without a single problem.

Active Braking Mosfet

A long lasting option made to increase performance.

As the name says, besides protecting your trigger contacts, this mosfet has Active Braking. Active Braking is a feature that makes an AEGs gearbox always do a complete cicle. That way, the parts inside the gearbox are submited to less stress making them last longer. This mosfet was designed to have minimal wear on the motor as well as to avoid over heating making it a reliable mosfet with excelent performance.

PTW Mosfet

The mosfet upgrade for your TW.

With a brand new and innovative design, this mosfet is made to increase the performance maintaining the reliability. Engineered to keep working without problems where others break, this mosfet isn't a just a replacement part, it's an upgrade.With it's size it makes it possible to carry the battery inside the buffer-tube. It's compatible with Systema, Celcius and G&D (newer generations) branded TWs. Functional prototype in test fase.

Spring Spacer

Precision power for your sniper.

Simple solution to make small increases in the power of your airsof sniper rifle. Compatible with vsr-10 and aps-2 systems, each spacer gives an 15-20fps increase in power (value may vary due to the other internal components of your rifle). It's a perfect solution for those trying to get a precise power number without having to install a stronger spring. These spacers are made of hardened metal and are available in packs of 2.
