About Us

NO BULLSHIT LAB is a one-of-a-kind accelerator for makers and crafters, focused on developing innovative product ideas and helping them become promising companies.
NO bullshit included ;-)

Team

A team devoted to product creation. With several years of experience developing hardware and businesses, we're ready to mentor you to success!

Creation

Scale

Road Map

NO BULLSHIT Lab is a 6-month accelerator program that gives startups, makers and crafters the resources to create sustainable products. Ten projects at a time will be given the tools and connections to build and bring products to market.
