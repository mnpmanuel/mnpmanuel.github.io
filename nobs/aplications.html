FAQ

What is No Bullshit Lab?

NO BULLSHIT Lab is a 6-month accelerator program that gives startups, makers and crafters the resources to create sustainable products. Ten projects at a time will be given the tools and connections to build and bring products to market.

What is an accelerator?

A startup accelerator is a fixed-term, cohort-based program, that includes mentorship and educational components normally culminating in a public pitch event or demo day

What are you looking for?

We invest in great entrepreneurs pursuing large market opportunities with products that delight their customers. We are particularly interested in technically leaning teams in the connected device space and business models with recurring revenue potential.

What are you not looking for?

We're open to a wide variety of markets and stages, but tend to stay away from products with long development cycles such as cars and markets that require multi-year regulatory approval such as surgical robots. Companies should also be targeting a significant market opportunity.

How can you help me?

We've developed an awesome educational program, in close partnership with NOVA School of Business & Economics, that's going to help you inprove your product and make it scalable. In addition to that, we also have a team of mentors and technical advisors with vast experience in all the key areas that you'll need. You'll also have access to all the tools you need to prototype and market test your product.

What resources will I have access to?

We'll provide you with access to fully equiped prototyping facilities and free prototyping materials. You'll also have access to a selection of factories waiting to mass produce your product.

How will you help me grow my product?

In adition to the access to prototyping facilities and factories, you'll also have access to retail channels, as well as crowdfunding experts and the best hardware VCs.

Will you have space to work?

You'll have access to 6 months of office space, including the prototyping facilities.

Are you open to international teams?

Absolutely.

Do you help with transportation and accomodation?

Yes. We'll provide accomodation and transportation to the participants in the accelerator

What kind of business models do you get most excited about?

We look for business models which provide high customer lifetime value, usually through software or software-like recurring revenue business models. We are interested in companies working both on consumer devices and within the business-to-business/enterprise connected devices space.

Can you help me protect my IP?

During the accelerator you'll have access to accounting, legal and patent services.

Where do I send my NDA before I pitch you my startup idea?

Like the vast majority of venture investors, we typically do not sign non-disclousure agreements. Brad Feld has a great writeup on his blog about why most VCs can't sign NDAs.

We've been operating our startup for awhile and have raised some capital. Can you still help?

Oftentimes yes. If you need help scaling, prototyping or producing your product, we're here for you.
